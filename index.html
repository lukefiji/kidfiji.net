Hello, I'm Luke Fiji.
I grew up learning how to use computers at a very young age experimenting with Microsoft Paint. In my teenage years, I discovered that Photoshop was installed on my computer and began to tinker around with it, following step-by-step tutorials online, eventually leading to my passion for graphic design. When MySpace came around, I applied my knowledge of Photoshop to customize my profile, learning a bit of HTML and CSS along the way. I soon began to appreciate the intricacies of web design and decided to pursue an informatics degree, specializing in human-computer interaction, at UC Irvine. With my bachelor's degree now in hand, I enjoy designing graphics and websites, for fun.
